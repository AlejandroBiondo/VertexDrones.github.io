About Vertex Drones

Vertex Drones Company is a construction focused drone service provider based in Miami, Florida. We specialize in aerial surveying, mapping, progress monitoring, and inspection services that improve safety, visibility, and decision making on active job sites.

We combine state of the art drone technology with construction experience to help project managers, field engineers, and developers detect issues early, reduce rework, and stay on schedule and budget.

Our mission

To enhance construction performance and safety by delivering reliable, data driven aerial services through the latest drone technology.

Our vision

To become the most trusted drone surveying partner for the construction industry by providing accurate insights, fast turnarounds, and exceptional service.

Services

Vertex Drones provides end to end aerial data services tailored to construction and civil engineering. Deliverables are ready for use in BIM, Procore, and CAD environments.

Aerial surveying

High resolution topographic maps, elevation models, and orthomosaics that support planning, permitting, and site layout.

Progress monitoring

Weekly or milestone based flights that document site progress and support schedule tracking, pay applications, and owner reporting.

Mapping and 3D modeling

Detailed 2D and 3D models using photogrammetry and LiDAR, compatible with AutoCAD, Civil 3D, and BIM platforms.

Volume calculations

Accurate cut, fill, and stockpile volume calculations that streamline earthwork estimates and help control project costs.

Thermal and safety inspections

Thermal and visual inspections to identify safety risks, leaks, insulation issues, and material inconsistencies without exposing workers to height risks.

Proposal and estimating support

Preconstruction flights for site intelligence, access planning, and quantity verification that strengthen proposals and bids.
